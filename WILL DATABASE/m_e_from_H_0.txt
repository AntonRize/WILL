The equation becomes:
\begin{equation}

$$ \text{RHS} = \frac{c}{H_0} \cdot \frac{m_e c}{\alpha \hbar} = \frac{m_e c^2}{H_0 \alpha \hbar} $$

\begin{equation}

$$ m_e^3 = \frac{(\alpha \hbar c) \cdot (H_0 \alpha \hbar)}{G c^2} = \frac{H_0 (\alpha \hbar c)^2}{G c^3} $$
